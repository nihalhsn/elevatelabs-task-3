MEDIUM SEVERITY VULNERABILITIES
-------------------------------------------------

1) SSL Certificate Cannot Be Trusted
   - Plugin ID: 51192
   - CVSSv3: 6.5
   - Description:
     The SSL certificate presented by the host cannot be fully trusted.
     This may be due to self-signed certificates, expired certificates,
     or certificates signed by untrusted authorities.
   - Risk:
     Connections to this host may be intercepted or spoofed.
   - Remediation:
     Install a valid SSL certificate signed by a trusted CA.

2) SMB Signing Not Required
   - Plugin ID: 57608
   - CVSSv3: 5.3
   - Description:
     The remote SMB server does not require SMB signing.
   - Risk:
     Allows Man-in-the-Middle attacks on SMB connections.
   - Remediation:
     Enable SMB signing on the system or domain policy.
